About us

BASE is a custom SaaS apps service provided by Glue Labs.

Glue Labs is a fully distributed team of developers who have built SaaS apps for the world’s top VC funded startups and also for 10,000 people plus large enterprise customers.

Glue Labs is led by our founder, Vishal Chandra. He is an alumni from IIT Delhi and a serial tech entrepreneur with a keen love for technology. His first startup was into wireless hardware tech and developed one of the world’s first 1 Gbps wireless systems.

Vishal, loves his coffee and helping startup founders be successful.
